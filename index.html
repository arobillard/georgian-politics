Character Creation

For this campaign, PCs will be starting at level 5. As we are assuming the PCs have some prior adventuring experience, they will be allowed to start with extra upgrades and equipment beyond the standard starting equipment for level 1. When creating your PC, use the standard PC build and add the following:

  • Up to 500gp worth of non-magical equipment upgrades*
  • Up to 3 points of upgrades from the Upgrades Table
*PCs may keep a maximum of 100gp of leftover gold after making upgrades.

Don't forget to add your character to the DnD Beyond campaign!
